About us

Our Mision & Objective

The University Day Care Center (Utkalitaa) is determined to provide the quality of day care facility for our university staffs in a safe, nurturing and educational environment. Our center is committed to meet the social and emotional needs of their child. We will continuously empower parents to advocate for their children. Our hope is to strengthen the family bond by utilizing community resources to support the families of our Center.

The objective of the Day Care Centre and play school is to provide the Faculty, Officers, Non-Teaching Staff of Utkal University and children of outside neighboring Areas. The facility of keeping their children under good care during the working hourâs right inside the university campus. The members of the university community had long demanded establishment of a Day Care Centre in the campus so that they can work with peace of mind and can access their children quickly in case of emergency. Apart from taking good care of the enrolled children, one of the key objectives of the Centre is also to involve the children in various activities thereby enhancing their motor and cognitive skills. The Day Care Centre could turn out to be an important learning ground to teach the child a whole lot of lessons in socialization that could turn out to be invaluable in later life. We are dedicated to providing families with the best possible care for their children at the most affordable cost.

Our Goal

1.To assure parents that their children will be in a safe, healthy environment where staff will strive to meet emotional, social, and physical needs.

4.To provide a diverse range of developmentally appropriate activities, including but not limited to arts and crafts, self-directed play, field trips, dramatic play, indoor and outdoor physical activities, community service, reading, quiet time, and fostering an environment that encourages homework.
